Studentikoo Podcast

assignment

The aim of this project was to make a business model canvas for a start-up. We decided to do this for a podcast.

process

In this project it was important that research was done through different strategies to form a good picture of our idea to start up a podcast. We did this for example with a DESTEP Analyse and a SWOT analyse. With all the research I designed a Business Model Canvas. The 9 topics give an idea of what we need to start the podcast.
