Donrox

assignment

I spent my internship at Surf&Turf working on a cool project: redesigning the entire website for Donrox. I was part of the design team from start to finish, working on everything from the layout to the overall look and feel. Once we were happy with the design, we passed it on to the developers who built it using Shopify.

process

This was my first internship, and it was a total eye-opener to see how a company works and how they interact with clients. I got to be part of everything, from the first meetings with the client to the final handover. I learned so much about Figma during this project, and it's now my go-to design tool. We used a design system throughout the project, which kept everything really organized and made it easy to explain our decisions to the client..
