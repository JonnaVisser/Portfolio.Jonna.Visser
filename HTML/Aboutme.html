THIS IS ME

Hi there! Let me tell you a bit about myself. On November 19, 1994, I was Born in the charming, historic town of Doesburg in the east of the Netherlands. Since January 2022, I've got my own place in Amsterdam, where I'm fully immersed in my Communication & Multimedia Design (CMD) studies at the Amsterdam University of Applied Sciences (HVA).

After starting my degree at the HAN University of Applied Sciences (HAN) in Arnhem, I realized that the Amsterdam University of Applied Sciences offered a more tailored program for my interests. So, I made the move to Amsterdam and am now in my final year of this exciting program. Before diving into the world of design, I explored other paths. I first trained as a waitress and gained some experience in floor management. Then, I delved into Marketing & Communication. While I enjoyed my marketing studies, I felt the need to deepen my knowledge and pursue a higher education level.

Balancing my studies with a job as a waitress at Loetje Zuidas, I've developed strong interpersonal skills and a passion for creating positive experiences for others. In my free time, I love exploring the vibrant city of Amsterdam, finding peace in nature, and connecting with friends and family over good food and great company. Of course, there's nothing quite like curling up with a good Netflix series for some much-needed relaxation.

LEARNING GOALS

As a CMD student, I've had the opportunity to learn a great deal and develop my skills over the past few years. I've explored various directions within the program, including development, UI design, and UX design. I have concluded that UX design suits me the best and brings me the most enjoyment. Additionally, I've realized that I get a lot of energy from conducting research, which is why I'd like to delve deeper into UX research as well. My focus now is to continue immersing myself in the work of a UX designer and to become increasingly proficient in this field.

INTEREST
