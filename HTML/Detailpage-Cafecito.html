Cafecito

assignment

For this assignment I worked in a duo with Yujin. We had to come up with a solution for the problem not enough staff. We did this for Cafecito.

process

Yujin and I started by doing some research. In this research we did deskresearch and fieldresearch. For the fieldresearch we did some interviews with employees and customers of Cafecito. After the research we did some part with ideation. Here we put our problem, insights and our solution. After describing our solution we made some prototypes of our solution. Worked together with: Yujin Choi.
