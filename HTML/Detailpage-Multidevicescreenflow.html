Multi Device Screenflow

assignment

For this assignment I had to came up with an interactive experience for multiple devices. I have shown this a wireflow and in a screen flow after I did some research.

process

In this project I started brainstorming for an idea. After finding this idea, I wrote out a scenario and looked at what the most important purposes of the interactive experience are. I was able to write 3 job stories from this and these job stories have helped me a lot during the entire process in this project. Based on the job stories, I first looked at the other business who are working in the same industry as my idea, which is social media. after that I started my interactive experience by making a wireflow in at a low-fidelity level in Adobe XD. This wireflow gives the first picture of my interactive multi-device experience. In this process it was important for me to continue to see the flow as the main part. After making a wireflow a was able to make a great high-fidelity screenflow out of it. This screenflow was my end product this project.
