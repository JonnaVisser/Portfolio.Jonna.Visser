Jenny's Adventure:
Believe In Yourself

assignment

For this assignment I had to make my own story. I wrote this story using the steps of the hero's journey. With this story I had te make an animation with Adobe Animate

process

In this project I started by writing my story based on the 12 steps in the hero's journey. My story is about a girl who has to believe in herself and face her fears. After I wrote my story I made sketches of the most important frames of my story. All the objects in my animation I designed myself with Adobe Illustrator. I designed the object in a way it fits the look and feel of my story. After I had my story and the designs, I made the scenes for my animated story. I made my animated story in Adobe animate, but unfortunally I am not able anymore to open this story.
