MatchingApp Dogs At The Park

assignment

For this assignment I had to focus on front-end and back-end development. I learned the basic skills in back-end and expanded my knowledge of front-end. I had to do this by creating a matching app. For this matching app I did the whole front-end development and back-end development myself.

process

In a few weeks I went through a process of building the client-side and server-side of my matching app Dogs At The Park. On the server-side I started by setting up a server step by step. After that I also set up a database. On the client side I have been working on a progressive enhancement which has become a map in my matching app where the user can click on one of the parks. The second important thing on the client side was API's. I looked through a lot of API's and added some in my matching app. At the end of the process I put my Matching app in the cloud by running it with Heroku. The project and the process of it is on Github.
