Ommetje App

assignment

I worked on a case for the Ommetje app and made a redesign for the main flow of the Ommetje app. The two most important things I want to bring in the main flow is more motivation for the user and make it more joyful for the user. For these reasons I created Brainie. This character is a joyful way of motivating the user. Brainie is the character that work as a personal motivational coach in the app. In this assignment the research part was a lot smaller so that I could focus more on detailing my prototype with micro interactions and animations. With some added micro interaction and animation, I made the app more interesting for the target group and made it more modern and more fun to use.

process

After the briefing I started by making the user flow of the Ommetje app. I made some changes in the old flow, and this was the start of my process of changing the Ommetje app. With the flow and the briefing in mind I started the design process. I had a process of making the design for the different flows and I made a lot of iterations in the process. With all the information I kept redesigning At the end of the project, I made one more redesign based on some user tests. This whole redesign is tested by three users Paul, Rik, and Caroline. They did some findings. I was able to iterate with the findings and worked on some improvements at the end of the project. These improvements were small changes in the way the micro interactions and animations worked. With this new redesign I will present a Ommetje main flow with a joyful and motivational character.
